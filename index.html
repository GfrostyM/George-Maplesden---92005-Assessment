    • What is Biodiversity?

    Biodiversity in basic terms, is the variety of all life on Earth. This includes all living organisms such as animals, plants and other life.

    Biodiversity in New Zealand

    New Zealand/Aotearoa is a very biodiverse country as a whole. There are large areas of bush and forrest which contain various native birds plants and much more.
